Cup of Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff's Cup was a magical item created by Helga Hufflepuff, one of the founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Fun Facts
  • The emblematic animal is a badger, and yellow and black are its colours.
  • Hufflepuff corresponds roughly to the element of earth,[5] and it is for that reason that the House colours were chosen: yellow represented wheat, while black was emblematic of soil
